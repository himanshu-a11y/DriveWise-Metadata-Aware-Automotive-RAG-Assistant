BRAND: Tata
MODEL: Nexon
VERSION: 2026

[SECTION: engine and performance]
[PAGE: 2]
The Tata Nexon is available with a 1.2 litre turbo petrol engine producing around 120
horsepower and a 1.5 litre turbo diesel engine producing around 115 horsepower. A more
powerful petrol tune with around 125 horsepower is offered on select trims. Transmission
options include a 5-speed manual, a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed torque converter automatic,
and a 7-speed dual clutch automatic depending on the engine and trim chosen.

[SECTION: mileage and fuel efficiency]
[PAGE: 3]
The petrol manual variant returns close to 17.4 kilometres per litre. The diesel manual
variant is more fuel efficient, returning close to 24 kilometres per litre, making it one
of the more efficient diesel options in its class. The automatic petrol variant returns
around 17 kilometres per litre.

[SECTION: safety]
[PAGE: 4]
The Nexon comes standard with dual front airbags, and higher trims extend this to six
airbags. It includes electronic stability control, hill hold assist, and a tyre pressure
monitoring system. The Nexon has consistently scored a five star rating in Global NCAP
crash tests, and is known for its strong body structure.

[SECTION: dimensions]
[PAGE: 5]
The Nexon has a length of 3993 mm, a width of 1811 mm, and a height of 1616 mm. Ground
clearance is rated at around 209 mm, among the highest in its segment. Boot space is
382 litres, expandable when the rear seats are folded.

[SECTION: interior and comfort]
[PAGE: 6]
The interior includes a semi digital instrument cluster, an air purifier, automatic climate
control, and a wireless phone charger on higher trims. Rear seats offer decent under thigh
support, and the top trim includes ventilated seats.

[SECTION: infotainment and connectivity]
[PAGE: 7]
A 12.3 inch touchscreen infotainment system is offered on top trims, along with an
eight speaker JBL sound system. The Nexon also supports connected car features through
Tata's app, including remote lock and unlock, vehicle location tracking, and driving
behaviour reports.
