BRAND: Maruti Suzuki
MODEL: Baleno
VERSION: 2026

[SECTION: engine and performance]
[PAGE: 2]
The Maruti Baleno uses a 1.2 litre petrol engine producing around 90 horsepower. It is
offered with a 5-speed manual gearbox or a CVT automatic. A factory fitted CNG option is
also available, which lowers running cost but reduces peak power slightly.

[SECTION: mileage and fuel efficiency]
[PAGE: 3]
The manual petrol variant returns close to 22.9 kilometres per litre, and the CVT automatic
variant returns close to 22.9 kilometres per litre as well due to Maruti's mild hybrid
system. The CNG variant returns around 30.6 kilometres per kilogram, making it a strong
choice for buyers focused on running cost.

[SECTION: safety]
[PAGE: 4]
The Baleno offers up to six airbags on higher trims, along with electronic stability
control, hill hold assist, and a tyre pressure monitoring system. It has a three star
rating in Global NCAP crash tests for adult occupant protection.

[SECTION: dimensions]
[PAGE: 5]
The Baleno has a length of 3990 mm, a width of 1745 mm, and a height of 1500 mm. Ground
clearance is rated at around 170 mm. Boot space is 318 litres.

[SECTION: interior and comfort]
[PAGE: 6]
The cabin features a dual tone dashboard, automatic climate control on higher trims, and
a head up display unique to this segment. Rear seats are reasonably spacious for the car's
size, though ground clearance and cushioning are on the firmer side.

[SECTION: infotainment and connectivity]
[PAGE: 7]
A 9 inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay is
offered on top trims, along with a six speaker Arkamys tuned sound system. Suzuki
Connect allows remote vehicle tracking, geofencing, and driving score reports via a
smartphone app.
