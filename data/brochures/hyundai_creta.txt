BRAND: Hyundai
MODEL: Creta
VERSION: 2026

[SECTION: engine and performance]
[PAGE: 2]
The Hyundai Creta is offered with a 1.5 litre petrol engine producing around 115 horsepower,
and a 1.5 litre diesel engine producing around 116 horsepower. A turbo petrol option with
around 160 horsepower is also available for buyers who want stronger acceleration. Transmission
choices include a 6-speed manual, a CVT automatic (petrol), and a 6-speed automatic (diesel).
The turbo petrol version comes with a 7-speed dual clutch automatic. All engines meet BS6 Phase 2
emission norms.

[SECTION: mileage and fuel efficiency]
[PAGE: 3]
The 1.5 litre petrol manual variant returns close to 17 kilometres per litre under standard
test conditions. The diesel manual variant is more efficient, returning close to 20.2 kilometres
per litre. The CVT automatic petrol variant returns around 17.4 kilometres per litre. The turbo
petrol variant, being more powerful, returns a lower figure of around 16.8 kilometres per litre.

[SECTION: safety]
[PAGE: 4]
The Creta comes with up to six airbags across most variants, electronic stability control,
hill start assist, all wheel disc brakes on higher trims, tyre pressure monitoring, and a
360 degree camera system on top variants. It also offers Level 2 ADAS features such as
forward collision warning, lane keep assist, and adaptive cruise control on the top trim.
The car has a five star rating in Global NCAP crash tests for the latest generation.

[SECTION: dimensions]
[PAGE: 5]
The Creta has a length of 4330 mm, a width of 1790 mm, and a height of 1635 mm. The wheelbase
is 2610 mm, which contributes to good rear legroom. Ground clearance is rated at 190 mm,
which is useful for Indian road conditions. Boot space is 433 litres.

[SECTION: interior and comfort]
[PAGE: 6]
The cabin offers a dual tone dashboard, ventilated front seats on higher trims, a wireless
phone charger, an air purifier, and ambient lighting. Rear passengers get adjustable
headrests and a rear armrest with cup holders. The top variant also gets a panoramic sunroof.

[SECTION: infotainment and connectivity]
[PAGE: 7]
A 10.25 inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay
is offered on higher trims, along with a Bose premium sound system with eight speakers.
The Creta also supports Hyundai's connected car technology, allowing remote engine start,
vehicle status tracking, and geo-fencing alerts through a smartphone app.
